Leadership

Prepare the ones who will carry

Leadership here is not platform or status. It is weight-bearing service: people made trustworthy enough to protect others, guard truth, and multiply healthy order.

Entrust to faithful people who will be able to teach others also.

Character

Strength under pressure

The first question is not gifting but steadiness. Can this person remain humble, obedient, and clear when responsibility increases and attention follows?

Guarding

Protect what is sound

Leadership guards doctrine, keeps community from drifting, and recognizes what threatens unity. The work is quiet, disciplined, and costly in the right ways.

Multiplication

Raise others, do not gather admirers

Healthy leaders do not terminate the work in themselves. They identify, train, release, and continue building people who can serve the body with the same integrity.

Final Charge

Take your place in the body

The movement becomes durable when every lane is filled by people who are awake, faithful, and willing to bear their portion of the work.
