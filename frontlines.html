Front Lines

Where the call is first heard

This is the edge where names are noticed, burdens are named, and people meet the nearness of God with clarity instead of confusion.

Lift up a banner on the bare height.

Witness

Presence before persuasion

The front lines do not perform. They show up, see clearly, and speak truth with gravity. The first ministry is to remain awake enough to recognize the moment in front of you.

Invitation

An open door with weight on it

The invitation is not into hype. It is into repentance, surrender, prayer, and the beginning of belonging. The lane exists to bring people across the threshold.

Hand-off

No one is left at the edge

Encounter must lead somewhere. This lane is strongest when those who respond are immediately seen, welcomed, and walked into the next step of formation.

Next Step

Bring them in close

What begins at the edge must continue in relationship, teaching, correction, and care.
