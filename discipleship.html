Discipleship

Form what has been found

Discipleship is the slow, holy work of staying near enough for the Word to take root, for habits to change, and for obedience to become durable.

Teach them to observe all that I have commanded you.

Nearness

Life close enough to be seen

Formation does not happen at a distance. It requires nearness, honesty, accountability, and the patience to walk with people through repetition and resistance.

Doctrine

Truth that steadies the inner life

The aim is not information alone. It is truth becoming weight-bearing inside a person, correcting what is crooked and strengthening what is weak.

Practice

Prayer, obedience, endurance

Prayer rhythms, Scripture immersion, confession, service, and daily faithfulness belong here. The lane teaches people how to remain, not merely how to begin.

Next Step

Strengthen what must endure

People who are being formed also need covering, tools, and support that help them remain steady for the long work ahead.
